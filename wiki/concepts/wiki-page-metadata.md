# Wiki Page Metadata

## Summary

Wiki pages use frontmatter fields such as `areas`, `categories`, `tags`, `related`, `sources`, `sensitivity`, and `encryption` to support retrieval, review, provenance, and privacy controls. [SRC-0002]

## Key Points

- `areas` describe broad top-level contexts such as work, personal, research, admin, or finance. [SRC-0002]
- `categories` are hierarchical labels for multi-category organization. [SRC-0002]
- `tags` are looser descriptors, while `related` lists explicit links to connected pages. [SRC-0002]
- `sources` preserves provenance for synthesized claims. [SRC-0002]
