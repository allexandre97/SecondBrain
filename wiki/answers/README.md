# Answer Notes

Answer notes are generated, readable responses to user questions about the knowledge base. They are written as Markdown pages so they can be reviewed and read later in Obsidian.

Answer notes are useful for review, synthesis, and later reading, but they are not the only durable home for knowledge. When an answer uncovers reusable knowledge, missing context, a durable claim, a contradiction, or an open question, that material should also be written back to the relevant source, concept, question, claim, or tension pages.

Answer notes should preserve provenance, list the wiki pages and raw sources used, identify any durable wiki pages updated, and mark remaining gaps clearly.
